[Solo 2 - 3:30]

Wild! Reference: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WadeRHtuurc

I don't look at live footage when transcribing guitars (although I probably should and it might seem dumb not to, it's just not my preference).
The above link however captures a great deal of the typical OGRE "wailing blues" soloing style, many of the recyclable shapes and movements, and specific licks you might even find in other OGRE songs!

PS - I still learned this by ear and through my own intuitions to document the gist; by no means am I saying I attempted to translate the above video in any way, nor does it exactly match the studio recording.

When I vibrato big bends - especially on the 1st and 2nd strings - I actually like to bend up slightly *past* the correct pitch to catch the vibrato correctly. Sometimes you might also need to use the weight of the guitar pressing back against you to do so.
