[Solo 2 - 3:30]

Wild! Reference: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WadeRHtuurc

I don't look at live footage when transcribing guitars (although I probably should and it might seem dumb not to, it's just not my preference).
The above link however captures a great deal of the typical OGRE "wailing blues" soloing style since the demo tape days. You'll also see lots of recyclable shapes and movements, and specific licks you might even find in other songs and bands. 

PS - I still learned this by ear and through my own intuitions to document the gist; by no means am I saying I attempted to translate the above video in any way, nor does it exactly match the studio recording.
